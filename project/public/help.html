What is Hivemind?

An ultra-lightweight, one-page productivity homepage application. Get work done, without worrying about whether or not your browser is slowing down your productivity.

Who's it made by?

Christopher Raley, Johnny Wong, William Wong, and Dennis Yang.

What's it powered with?

As of now, the holy trinity of HTML/CSS/JavaScript, as well as: NewsAPI, SVGBackgrounds.com.
